Steak House

Excellent steak

Rib-Eye

Cook steak in sous-vide to get perfect cooked level
Do not forget to burn at the end of cooking

Ingrediants

  • Buy sous-vide & vaccum machine
  • Rib-Eye steak, 400g
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • thyme

How to Cook

  1. Season the salt and pepper on the meat on all sides
  2. put the meat in a sealing bag
  3. vacuum the bag in a vacuum machine
  4. Set the device to a temperature of 56 degrees for 4 hours in a fully water pot
  5. insert the bag with the steak in it
  6. open the bag
  7. burn the steak on super heat mangal
  8. enjoy
